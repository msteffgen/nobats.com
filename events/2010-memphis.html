The Story

Season 20 in Memphis

The Redbirds (Cardinals AAA franchise) were great hosts and loved having us. Thanks to our efforts and donors, No Bats raised enough money for 900 boys and girls to attend summer baseball and softball camps.

No trip to Memphis is complete without bowing to the King at Graceland. The Peabody duck parade was right next door too. To gain revenge on Angles for screwing up our shirts in San Diego two years before, we had the nipples cut out of all four uniforms with his preferred number and froze them in buckets of block ice.
