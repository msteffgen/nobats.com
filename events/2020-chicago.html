The Story

Season 30 at Guaranteed Rate Field (Postponed)

With great anticipation we looked forward to partnering with the White Sox, an event that now has been moved to 2022. Aside from the charity match (50 cents on each dollar) we would get from the McCormick Foundation, we were also working on partnering with the club to hold life skills seminars for beneficiaries of the ball club's many urban initiatives.

Despite the event being postponed due to the global COVID-19 pandemic, our commitment to charitable giving continued. The No Bats community demonstrated resilience and dedication to our mission, finding ways to support important causes even when we couldn't gather for our traditional baseball weekend.

The planned partnership with the White Sox represented an exciting new chapter in our club's history, building on our tradition of combining baseball with meaningful community impact. While we had to wait for safer times to realize this vision, the foundation was laid for future collaboration.
