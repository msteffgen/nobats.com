The Story

Season 34 at Savannah

No Bats was excited to team up with the Savannah Bananas for our 2024 event supporting the incredible work of Bananas Foster. This unique partnership perfectly combined our love of baseball with a meaningful cause that touches countless lives.

Bananas Foster is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to “Bringing Families Together” by celebrating the foster care community, while educating and inspiring others to get involved. Their mission aligns perfectly with our club's values of community service and being significant in the lives of others.

Celebrating Season 34 in Savannah, the No Bats Baseball Club was honored to support this extraordinary organization that works tirelessly to create stronger, more connected, and more visible families through what is typically an “under the radar” foster care system.

The Savannah Bananas' commitment to entertainment and community engagement made them an ideal beneficiary for our meaningful, heartwarming support.
